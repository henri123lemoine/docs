The goal of K-Scale Labs is to make humanoid robotics accessible to everyone. Stompy Mini is a small humanoid robot that that we've 3D printed with PLA to allow for hobbyists to download our files and print their own models.

Copy our current CAD model from onshape [here](https://cad.onshape.com/documents/50fe60f593f5c98e8f52552c/w/23cfc61ce40dd25ad76f414c/e/9c37b547deaa42ff258901fe).
